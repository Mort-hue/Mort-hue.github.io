📖 How to Play

  1. Click any card to flip it and reveal either a flag or a country name.
  2. Flip a second card — if both belong to the same country, they match!
    • Matched pairs stay face-up and glow green ✅
    • Unmatched pairs flip back after a short delay
    • Try to remember what you saw to find pairs faster
  3. Match all 18 pairs to win.
    • Fewer moves = better score
    • Your best time is saved for bragging rights
  4. Hit New Game to shuffle and play again.
